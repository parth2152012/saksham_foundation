About Us

Saksham Foundation was founded with a simple yet profound vision: to ensure every child, regardless of their background, has access to basic necessities and opportunities for a brighter future. We believe in nurturing young minds and providing a safe environment for them to grow and thrive.

For over a decade, we have been working tirelessly in various communities, focusing on education, health, and overall well-being. Our programs are designed to address the root causes of poverty and empower children to break the cycle through sustainable interventions.

We are a team of passionate volunteers, dedicated staff, and generous donors who share a common goal: to make a tangible difference in the lives of vulnerable children.

Our Mission

Education

Providing access to quality education, school supplies, and learning resources to foster intellectual growth.

Health & Nutrition

Ensuring children receive proper nutrition, medical care, and hygiene education for a healthy life.

Child Protection

Creating safe environments and advocating for policies that protect children from exploitation and abuse.
